# ☕ Brewlytics: From Beans to Business Insights

## 🚀 Project Story
Every cup of coffee tells a story — of customer choices, peak rush hours, and business performance.

This project transforms **149,116+ coffee shop transactions** into meaningful insights, helping answer one key question:

👉 *What truly drives coffee shop revenue and customer behavior?*

---

## 📊 The Challenge
Coffee shop businesses generate massive transactional data daily, but struggle to:

- Identify peak demand hours  
- Understand what customers actually prefer  
- Optimize product offerings  
- Improve store performance  
- Increase revenue through data-driven decisions  

---

## 🧠 The Approach
Using Excel and data visualization techniques, this project:

- Cleans and structures raw transactional data  
- Analyzes patterns across time, location, and product categories  
- Builds an interactive dashboard to visualize key business metrics  

---

## 📈 Key Business Discoveries

### ⏰ Morning Rush is Gold
Sales peak during early hours → strong demand for coffee-driven products  

### 📍 Location Matters
Hell’s Kitchen outperforms other locations → potential benchmark store  

### ☕ Product Dominance
Coffee & beverages contribute the majority of revenue  

### 📅 Friday = Revenue Driver
Highest order volume observed → ideal for promotions  

### 📦 Customer Preference
Large & Regular sizes dominate → pricing strategy opportunity  

### 📉 Hidden Opportunity
Evening sales drop → potential area for growth  

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

- 🎯 Launch targeted **morning promotions**  
- 🏪 Replicate success strategy of top-performing locations  
- 📦 Focus inventory on high-demand products  
- 📅 Run campaigns on high-performing days (Friday)  
- 📉 Boost evening sales using discounts or combos
